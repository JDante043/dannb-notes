# Rumus
## Sum / Difference Rule
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}f(x)\pm g(x)\\
= f'(x)\pm g'(x)
\end{align}$$
## Quotient Rule (uv)
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}=\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\\\\
= \frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}
\end{align}$$
## Reciprocal Rule
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}\frac{1}{f(x)}\\
= \frac{-f'(x)}{f(x)^2}
\end{align}$$
## Power Rule
$$\begin{align}
f(x) = x^n\\
f'(x) = n\cdot x^{n-1}
\end{align}$$
## Chain Rule
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx} f(x)^n\\
=n\cdot f'(x) \cdot f(x)^{n-1}
\end{align}$$
## Product Rule
$$\begin{align}
\frac{d}{dx}f(x)\cdot g(x)\\
= f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)
\end{align}$$
