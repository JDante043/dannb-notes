### Seri
$$\begin{align}
R = R_1 + R_2 + ...
\end{align}$$
### Paralel
$$\begin{align}
\frac{1}{R} = \frac{1}{R_1}+\frac{1}{R_2}+...
\end{align}$$
## Arus (A)
$$\begin{align}
I = I_1 = I_2\\
IR = IR_1 + IR_2
\end{align}$$

$$\begin{align}
V = V_1 + V_2\\
V=IR
\end{align}$$
